The Best Movies According to Cryo

My Top-3 Movies of all-time or at least that I recall while working on this project

Treasure Planet

Favourite animation movie since I was little, love the art, the soundtrack and of course the story, I mean, it is space+pirates and a lovely father-son relationship, who wouldn't love it.

Interstellar

My favourite movie overall I think, it has wonderful art, mesmerizing soundtracks, space, huge waves, a lovely father-daughter relationship... wait a sec, I'm seeing a pattern here...

The Bicentennial Man

This time there are no pirates or space, but just the majestic Robbie Williams in one of the movies that are sure to make me cry at the end.
